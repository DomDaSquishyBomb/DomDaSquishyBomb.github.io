My name is Dominic Le. I'm a 4th year undergraduate student at the Industrious University of Toronto, Canada. I double major in Cognitive Science (Computational Stream) and Psychology with a minor in Linguistics.

I also spent my free time going on hikes and building improvised shelters in the woods which is nice because there is a lot of green outside, and my favourite colour is green.

Nowadays, I spend my free time researching topics in cognitive science such as counterfactuals through behavioural experiments with adults and children and philsophy independent study. I still play music from time to time and I like to write about the history of DIY music movements.
