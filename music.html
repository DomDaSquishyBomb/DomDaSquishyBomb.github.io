Music

of Dominic T. Le

Music is a big passion of mine! My parents played me a large variety of music they liked throughout my childhood; My dad took me to guitar lessons at 8; I started writing joke songs when I was 11 and then sad songs when I was 13; And now, I write songs that belong to genres which I will never admit to... Currently I'm in a post-weezercore, garage-esque, post-emo-esque, nature loving band called Derwin.

I used to writes songs for and play in Nanaimo's Space Disco Band. Check out our 2022 album "First Time" on Bandcamp with lyrics and everything! Also, here's a website I made for the band back in its prime.

In compositionally dire times followed by emotional intensity, I publish songs to my SoundCloud. Although these emotions are mostly unrelatble to me now, they meant the world to me then, and that's so worth noting. Sometimes, I go by DJ Dinosaurus.
