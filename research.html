Research

by Dominic T. Le

Background

Currently at the University of Toronto, I'm working with Professor Patricia Ganea at the Language and Learning Lab , working closely with Ioana Grosu, helping design experiments researching counterfactual reasoning in children. In fact, I'd say my focus of research right now is counterfactual reasoning and its related mental representations. I want to get a good understanding of how time is woven into human cognition and I'm having blast reading papers from all cognitive sciences!
