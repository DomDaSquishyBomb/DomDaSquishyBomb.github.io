If I was Mr. Fantastic

Sep 30, 2025

Why doesn't Mr. Fantastic ever go goo form?
You know, like a big blob of goo?
He could pass through anything!
And be so bouncy!
If I was Mr. Fantastic,
I would go goo form all the time.
Man, I would so addicted to being a big blob of goo.
I would just be like,
"Hey, I'm Mr. Fantastic,
check out my goo form!"
And then I would just be like,
"Whoa, look at me, I'm a big blob of goo!"
I would be so addicted to going being a blob...
My team would probably alienate me.
It's okay though,
Because I'd be so wrapped up in my solo goo adventures that I wouldn't even notice.
Then, I would probably have to get a new moniker,
With a new set of mannerisms,
Like, a German accent,
And get into techno music or something...
But I wouldn't learn German, or anything, (cause I'm too busy being a blob of goo).
Maybe after a while, maybe on Saturday afternoon, someone would recognize me and be like,
"What happened to you, Mr. Fantastic?!"
And I'd be like, (in a Hollywood German accent),
"I'm no longer Mr.Fantastic,
Unts Unts Unts Unts,
I'm Mr. Goo-d".
