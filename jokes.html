Confucius Personal Assistant

Nov 26, 2025

Imagine that Confucius has been transported to modern times, is learning English, and that I am his personal assistant.
Confucius is seating on the toilet and says to me:
"Assistant, can you pass me the uh uh paper uh uh"
"The paper, Tao?" I say
He replies "No, I am Confucius."

If I was Mr. Fantastic

Sep 30, 2025

Why doesn't Mr. Fantastic ever go goo form?
You know, like a big blob of goo?
He could pass through anything!
And be so bouncy!
If I was Mr. Fantastic,
I would go goo form all the time.
Man, I would so addicted to being a big blob of goo.
At first, I would be like,
"Hey, I'm Mr. Fantastic,
and this situation calls for my goo form!"
And then eventually, I would just show up to fights ready to goo
I would be so addicted to going goo form...
My team would start hating me, and eventually alienate me
"Sorry, Reed, it's just starting to feel like we're water and oil."
Of course, this is a very ambiguous callout coming from the Human Torch.
Nevertheless, I'd be exiled and lose all of my lab funding.
I would probably have to get a new gig.
I would probably have to get a new moniker.
...
Maybe I'd get into techno music?
Maybe spend my savings to fly to Berlin?
...
Maybe after a while, maybe at some Berlin rave, a local will approach me to say
"Hi, I loved you then as Mr. Fantastic, and I love you no less now as Mr. Goo-d"
